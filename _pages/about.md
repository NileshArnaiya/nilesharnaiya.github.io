## My Journey 🚀

I learned to code at 17 – late by today’s standards. This sparked a passion for lifelong learning. I taught myself through platforms like edX, Udacity, and Coursera (when they were just starting!).  
After failing 12th grade, I bounced back by diving into internships, self-taught skills, and exploring the tech world. Bangalore became my second home, where I soaked up knowledge, collected swag, and ate a lot of free pizza at tech meetups.  

I’ve held various roles – some successful, others not so much. But every experience shaped my path. I even taught for a semester in South Africa while pursuing my Master’s degree.
